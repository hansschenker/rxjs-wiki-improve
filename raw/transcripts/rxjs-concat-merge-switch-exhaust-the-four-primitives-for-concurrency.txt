## The Pattern

| Primitive | + Map | = Operator | Behavior |
|-----------|-------|------------|----------|
| **Concat** | Map | `concatMap` | Sequential - one at a time |
| **Merge** | Map | `mergeMap` | Parallel - all together |
| **Switch** | Map | `switchMap` | Cancel - latest wins |
| **Exhaust** | Map | `exhaustMap` | Ignore - first wins |
