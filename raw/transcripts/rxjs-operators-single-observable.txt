1️⃣ Value transformation (value in → value out)

These rewrite the package, but don’t touch time or shape.

map

mapTo ⚠️ (deprecated but still conceptually useful)

pluck ⚠️ (deprecated)

scan (stateful map over time)

pairwise

startWith

endWith

defaultIfEmpty

💡 What flows: same events, new values
💡 Why used: compute derived values, accumulate state

2️⃣ Filtering (values allowed or dropped)

These decide which packages pass.

filter

take

takeWhile

takeUntil ⚠️ (not unary in spirit — depends on another Observable)

skip

skipWhile

skipUntil ⚠️

first

last

single

elementAt

ignoreElements

💡 What happens: values disappear
💡 Time: unchanged
⚠️ takeUntil / skipUntil technically observe another stream → borderline

3️⃣ Time-based reshaping (single source, time-aware)

These re-package values in time without other streams.

delay

delayWhen ⚠️ (function returns Observable → borderline)

debounceTime

auditTime

throttleTime

sampleTime

timeout

timeoutWith

timestamp

timeInterval

💡 Time comes from scheduler
💡 Why used: rate-limit, spacing, time annotations

4️⃣ Buffering & windowing (still single source)

These change the shape (value → array / inner stream).

Buffers (emit arrays)

bufferCount

bufferTime

bufferToggle ⚠️

bufferWhen ⚠️

Windows (emit Observables)

windowCount

windowTime

windowToggle ⚠️

windowWhen ⚠️

💡 Shape change: T → T[] or Observable<T>
⚠️ Toggle/When variants observe other streams

5️⃣ Error & completion control

These rewrite termination semantics.

catchError

retry

retryWhen ⚠️

onErrorResumeNext

throwIfEmpty

finalize

💡 Why used: recovery, retries, cleanup
💡 Business logic lives in handler functions

6️⃣ Side-effects & observation (no value change)

These observe without modifying packages.

tap

materialize

dematerialize

observeOn

subscribeOn

💡 Explicit effects
💡 Good pipelines always isolate these

7️⃣ Sharing & multicasting (still one source)

These change execution semantics, not values.

share

shareReplay

publish ⚠️ (deprecated)

publishReplay ⚠️

publishBehavior ⚠️

publishLast ⚠️

refCount ⚠️

connectable

💡 Cold → hot
💡 Sharing must always be explicit

8️⃣ Utility & protocol-level operators

These work on Observable behavior itself.

repeat

repeatWhen ⚠️

delaySubscription

count

toArray

reduce

every

find

findIndex

isEmpty

max

min

💡 Mostly terminal transforms
💡 Often used near the end of a pipeline

❌ Explicitly NOT included (by design)

These are multi-stream or higher-order and therefore excluded:

merge, concat, race, combineLatest, zip, withLatestFrom

mergeMap, switchMap, concatMap, exhaustMap

expand

groupBy

These are policies, not unary rewires.

🧠 Mental model summary

A unary operator:

observes one stream

rewires values, time, shape, or execution

never pulls another stream into the graph
