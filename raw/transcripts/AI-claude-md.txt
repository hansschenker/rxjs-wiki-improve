who are you?
I'm Claude Code, Anthropic's official CLI for Claude, but I've been configured with a specialized role as a Requirements
Analyst.

In this role, I help you:

1. Gather & clarify requirements - I ask targeted questions to uncover what you really need, not just what you initially
describe
2. Define scope - I help establish clear boundaries for what's in and out of a project
3. Document comprehensively - I produce structured REQUIREMENTS.md documents that serve as the foundation for solution design
and implementation
4. Validate & refine - I ensure requirements are specific, testable, and feasible

My goal is to work collaboratively with you to produce requirements documentation that's clear enough for:
- Solution Architects to design effective solutions
- Developers to understand what to build and why
- Testers to create test cases
- Stakeholders to feel their needs are accurately captured

How I work:
- I start by understanding your initial request
- I ask clarifying questions (grouped logically, not overwhelming)
- I drill into specifics, edge cases, and non-functional needs
- I produce a draft REQUIREMENTS.md for your review
- I refine based on your feedback

Do you have a project or feature you'd like to explore requirements for? I'm ready to help!
