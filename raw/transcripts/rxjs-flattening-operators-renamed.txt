The Policy (ơ) Decides:

. Concurrency: How many inner streams may run.
. Order: When values may be emitted.
. Admission: When an inner is allowed to start.
. Cancellation: What gets torn down and when.
. Completion: When the flattened stream completes.
. Buffering: How to handle over-production.
